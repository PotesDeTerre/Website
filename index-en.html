Who are we?

Potes de terre is an association of the Supaéro For Earth club. It allows students to buy fruits and vegetables from local producers. The products are fresh, seasonal, and organic, and are sold at the price set by the supplier.

What do we sell?

  • Fruit and vegetable basket (whole or half)
  • Fruit-only basket (whole or half)

How to buy?

Every weekend, the composition of the baskets is published. To buy fruits and vegetables, simply place your order before the following Monday at 8 PM. The distribution takes place on Thursday at 1 PM at the MDE.
