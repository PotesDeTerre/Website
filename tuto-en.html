Welcome to the payment page!

Potes de terre uses Lydia® for its payments.

Why?

Potes de terre is committed to reselling at the price set by the supplier and therefore makes no profit. Lydia® is the ideal solution for fee-free payments.

How to pay?

  1. Install Lydia®
  2. Open the app. Enter the amount you want to send, then click Send.
  3. Click Enter a number / email
  4. Enter the address: potesdeterre@gmail.com
  5. Fill in the following information:
    • The price: calculate based on what you are ordering:
      • Fruit and vegetable basket: €12
      • Half fruit and vegetable basket: €6
      • Fruit basket: €10
      • Half fruit basket: €5
    • Your first and last name.
    • What you want to order.
  6. Choose your payment method, and confirm.
